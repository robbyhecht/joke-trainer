Joke Trainer Guidebook

Save Your Favorite Jokes

If there's a joke you find hilarious and want to remember for later, you can add it to your own collection of favorite jokes. Once you're logged in, just press the "add to favorites" button beneath any joke to save!

Train Your Brain

The Trainer page takes you from funny to functional. You'll see all your favorite jokes, but this time with only a hint on the front and the whole joke on the back. You can use these flashcards to help you memorize the jokes and become a full blown entertainer!

Add Your Own Jokes

If you've got some real zingers that you'd love to work into your act, you can add as many as you'd like. They'll appear with the rest of the library anytime you're logged into your account!
